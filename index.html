About Us

Registered originally as a partnership under the name Jimeno and Associates, the firm is now called JIMENO COPE & DAVID LAW OFFICES with business address at 37th Floor, Rufino Pacific Tower 6784 Ayala Avenue cor. V.A. Rufino St., Makati City.

We are a full-service law firm engaged in the practice of the major fields of law such as corporate, labor, litigation of civil and criminal cases, family relations, immigration, trademarks and patents as well as special projects involving tie-up or close coordination with government instrumentalities.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Jimeno Cope & David Law Offices is committed to providing quality legal services to its clients in an environment that fosters teamwork and professionalism. We strive for excellence in everything we do and treat our clients' cases as if they were our own.

Our Vision

At Jimeno Cope & David Law Offices we work as a family. We are composed of dedicated lawyers and staff who work as a team towards the fulfillment of our clients' legal needs.

Our Services

JIMENO COPE & DAVID LAW OFFICES is a full-service law firm providing comprehensive legal solutions to both corporate and individual clients across a wide range of practice areas.

Corporate Law

Formation, contracts, compliance, mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures, and general corporate advisory services.

Litigation

Representation in civil and criminal disputes with strategic advocacy and courtroom expertise.

Labor Law

Employment matters, labor disputes, compliance with labor regulations, and workplace policy development.

Family Relations

Adoption, annulment of marriage, probate of wills, settlement of estates, and family succession planning.

Immigration Law

Immigration services, naturalization, visa processing, and international mobility solutions.

Intellectual Property

Trademark and patent registration, infringement claims, unfair competition disputes, and IP protection.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Arbitration and mediation services. Managing Partner is an international commercial arbitrator and mediator.

International Law

Cross-border transactions, international commercial matters, and foreign legal representation.

Environmental Law

Environmental compliance, regulatory matters, and sustainability-focused legal advisory.

Estate Planning

Estate planning, will preparation, probate administration, and settlement of estates.

Notarization Services

Notarization of deeds, instruments, last Wills and Testaments, and other legal documents.

Special Projects

Customized legal solutions for mergers & acquisitions, joint ventures, and unique business needs.
